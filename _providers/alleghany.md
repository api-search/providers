description: Alleghany Corporation was a holding company engaged in property and casualty reinsurance and insurance through its subsidiaries, with additional investments in non-financial businesses. In October 2022, Berkshire Hathaway completed its $11.6 billion acquisition of Alleghany Corporation, adding $14 billion in insurance float and approximately $8.8 billion in gross premiums written. Alleghany now operates as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway.
