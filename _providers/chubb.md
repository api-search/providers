Chubb offers commercial and personal property and casualty insurance, personal accident and supplemental health insurance, reinsurance, and life insurance. The company manages more than $200B in global assets, paid $21B in claims (2023), and offers more than 200 insurance products and services. Key personal lines include homeowners, auto, valuables (jewelry, arts, wine), boat and yacht, pet (through a Healthy Paws partnership), and travel insurance. Commercial lines include small business, accident and health, commercial package, cyber, management liability, marine, professional liability, workers' compensation, and industry-specialty programs (construction, financial institutions, healthcare, life sciences, manufacturing, real estate, technology, climate). Chubb Studio is
  the company's embedded insurance technology platform offering APIs and SDKs for partners to integrate Chubb insurance products into their own digital experiences.
